## Context

Customers in regulated industries need a tamper-evident record of every access to a secret. A mutable table is the wrong shape — we need the full history, not just current state.

## Decision

Audit events are appended to an immutable log and projected into queryable read models. The log, not the projection, is the source of truth.

## Consequences

- Complete, replayable history satisfies compliance review.
- New reports are just new projections over existing events.
- Projections add eventual-consistency lag the UI must account for.
