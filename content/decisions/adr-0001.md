## Context

The team is growing past the point where architectural context lives reliably in people's heads. New engineers repeatedly ask why a given choice was made, and the answers are scattered across chat threads, design docs, and tribal memory.

## Decision

We will keep a log of Architecture Decision Records as Markdown files alongside the code. Each record captures the context, the decision, and its consequences, and is never edited after acceptance — superseding decisions get a new record that links back.

## Consequences

- Decisions become reviewable in pull requests, next to the change they motivate.
- The log is append-only, so history stays honest.
- Requires a small amount of discipline to write one before merging significant changes.
