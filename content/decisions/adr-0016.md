---
title: "Replace REST webhooks with an event bus"
date: 2025-07-20
status: proposed
tags: [api, events]
summary: "Offer a durable event bus for integrators instead of best-effort outbound webhooks."
---

## Context

Outbound webhooks fail silently when a customer endpoint is down, and we have no good story for replay or ordering. Integrators routinely miss events.

## Decision

Publish domain events to a durable, partitioned event bus that integrators subscribe to, with retention and replay. Webhooks become a thin adapter on top.

## Consequences

- Integrators get ordered, replayable delivery with backpressure.
- We expose and document a public event schema we must version carefully.
- Smaller integrators may find a subscription model heavier than a simple webhook.
