## Context

Each service emits telemetry in a different proprietary format, and switching observability vendors would mean re-instrumenting everything from scratch.

## Decision

All services instrument with OpenTelemetry and export through a collector. The storage backend becomes a swappable detail behind the collector.

## Consequences

- One instrumentation standard across every service.
- Changing vendors is a collector config change, not a code change.
- The collector is new shared infrastructure to run and scale.
