## Context

The passive region in ADR-0009 is expensive idle capacity, and a manual failover still means minutes of downtime. We now have the tooling to consider concurrent writes.

## Decision

Move to active-active regions with conflict-handling at the data layer, routing users to their nearest healthy region. Failover becomes a non-event.

## Consequences

- Standby capacity starts doing real work and failover is near-instant.
- Conflict resolution and data locality become first-class, hard problems.
- Not every workload can tolerate multi-primary writes; some stay pinned. Would supersede ADR-0009.
