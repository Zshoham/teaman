date: 2025-01-15

summary: "Partition tenants into independent cells so a failure or noisy neighbour is contained."
---

## Context

As single-tenant blast radius grew, one tenant's runaway load or a bad migration could degrade everyone. Large customers also want hard isolation guarantees.

## Decision

Tenants are assigned to self-contained cells, each a full stack slice. A routing layer maps a tenant to its cell; cells share no request-path state.

## Consequences

- A failure is contained to one cell, not the whole fleet.
- Migrations and rollouts can proceed cell by cell.
- Routing, capacity planning, and per-cell observability add operational complexity.
