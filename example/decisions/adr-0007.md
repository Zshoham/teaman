## Context

We are outgrowing single-VM deploys and need rolling updates, health-based restarts, and horizontal autoscaling without building that machinery ourselves.

## Decision

Deploy onto a managed Kubernetes offering. Workloads are declared as manifests in the monorepo and reconciled by GitOps.

## Consequences

- Self-healing and autoscaling come from the platform.
- The team must invest in Kubernetes fluency and guardrails.
- Local development needs a lighter-weight story than full clusters.
